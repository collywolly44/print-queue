Import & Settings

Load new orders · manage data
Load CSV
Sync mode (default): upload each CSV as a complete snapshot of pending orders. Any order that was present before but isn't in the new CSV is treated as shipped — its parts are automatically subtracted from inventory. New orders get added. Use Append only if you're loading partial data and don't want anything subtracted.

Data Management
Backup
Download a full backup of all orders, parts library, and printed history as JSON, or send it to yourself by email.
Email opens your mail app with the backup pasted in. For large backups, use Share / Send to… instead (attaches as a file).
Auto-save history
The app keeps automatic snapshots of your data (up to 2, every 20 minutes) as you work. If you ever accidentally delete or break something, restore from a recent snapshot here. Snapshots are kept in this browser only — download a backup for off-device safety.
Danger zone
Wipe everything and start fresh. Inventory adjustments happen automatically as you print and as orders ship — there's no separate "printed" state to reset.
